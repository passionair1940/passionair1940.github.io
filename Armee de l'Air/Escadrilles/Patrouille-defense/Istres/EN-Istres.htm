Historical

The Patrol of Istres is created May 10, 1940 to protect the region of Montpellier-Salon-Istres. The unit is attached to Patrol Group N° 4: Two Patrols on Koolhoven FK58 in Istres and Salon and one on Morane MS406 in Clermont. The lack of equipment will call into question the planned organization: the Patrol of Montpellier is preserved but the other two are gathered in Salon and form a group with the two Patrols of Clermont.

There will be no FK58 to cover Istres. This coverage will be carried out by morane MS406. On June 8, a new organization is set up: the Istres patrol, with that of Montpellier, will create the East Subgroup of the ZOAA. But in fact, nothing happens as planned despite the request on June 15, to detach as soon as possible 6 MS 406 from the EAA301 of Toulouse and two others from Montpellier to equip the Patrol of Istres.

Thus the 6 missions requested on June 23 and the following two on June 24 can not be done ... very little information on this patrol, but it seems that the pilots are French.
