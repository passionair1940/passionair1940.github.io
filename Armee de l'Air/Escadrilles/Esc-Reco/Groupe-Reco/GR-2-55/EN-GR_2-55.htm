Reconnaissance Group GR II/55
3rd Squadron
4th Squadron

Historical

On the date of the outbreak of the German attack of May 10, 1940, the GR II / 55 is attached to the Air Cooperation Forces of the ZOAA (Zone of Air Operations Alps) and is assigned to the General Reserve.
