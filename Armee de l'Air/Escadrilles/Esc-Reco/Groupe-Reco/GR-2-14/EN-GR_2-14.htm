Historical

This Group was created on 05 May 1940

On the date of the outbreak of the German attack of May 10, 1940, the GR II / 14 belongs to the Air Cooperation Forces of the ZOAA (Zone of Air Operations Alps) and is attached to the Army of the Alps.
