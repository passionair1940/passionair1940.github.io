Night fighter squadron ECN IV/13

Historical

The ECN IV / 13 is formed from the 4th Squadron of GCN II / 13 in January 1940

On May 10, 1940, the ECN III / 13 is attached to the Night Hunting Group of the ZOAN (North Air Operations Area)
