Fighter Group GC I/8
1st Squadron (Ex 3C2)
2nd Squadron (Ex 3C3)

Historical

The I / 8 fighter group, formed on January 1, 1936, consisted of two squadrons that were originally Navy squadrons: the 3C2 (winged trident) being the first squadron of the group and the 3C3 (jumping lion) the second .

At wartime, the GC I / 8 is still equipped with DEWOITINE D501 and D510. The first BLOCH MB152 will arrive only from October 18, 1939.

On the date of the outbreak of the German attack of May 10, 1940, GC I / 8 is attached to Group No. 22 of the "Reserved Air Forces" of the ZOAE (East Air Operations Area).

The Group is definitively dissolved in November 1942.

Itinerary of the GC I/8

 

Aircrafts

The GC I/8 was equipped since its creation in Janvier 1936, following aircrafts :

- Dewoitine D501 / D510 until December 1939
- Potez 631
- Bloch MB151 / 152 et 155 from November 1939
- Dewoitine D520
The used aircrafts are described in the accessible file by the link below. You will find Nb of series, Registration, date of affectation to the group, and fate of the plane there.

In this list are identified :

    - 7 Dewoitine D501
    - 1 Dewoitine D510
    - 1 Potez 631
    - 3 Bloch MB151
    - 121 bloch MB152
    - 16 bloch MB155
