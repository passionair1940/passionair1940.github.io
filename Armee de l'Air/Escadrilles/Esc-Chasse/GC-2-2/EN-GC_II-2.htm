Fighter Group GC II/2
3rd Squadron (Ex SPA 65)
4th Squadron (Ex SPA 57)

Historical

The GC II / 2 was created at the end of 1933. The Group received its MORANE MS406 in the summer of 1939, and will do the whole campaign of France with this aircraft.

On the date of the outbreak of the German attack of May 10, 1940, the GC II / 2 belongs to the Fighting Group No. 23 of the ZOAN (North Air Operations Area).

In early June, part of the Group is seconded to the Lognes-Emerianville field to participate, with GC I / 6, in ground attack missions against German armored personnel. These attacks were very deadly for disappointing results.

He remains in France after the Armistice and will be dissolved in August 1940

Aircrafts

The GC II/2 was equipped since its creation at the end of 1933, following aircrafts :

- Nieuport-Delage Nid 622
- Dewoitine D500 / D501
- Morane-Saulnier MS406
The used aircrafts are described in the accessible file by the link below. You will find Nb of series, Registration, date of affectation to the group, and fate of the plane there.

In this list are identified :

    - 1 Nieuport-Delage Nid 622

    - 2 Dewoitine D501
