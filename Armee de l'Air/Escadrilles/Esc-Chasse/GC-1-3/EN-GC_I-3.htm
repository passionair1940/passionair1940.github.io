Fighter Group GC I/3
1st Squadron (Ex SPA 88)
2nd Squadron (Ex SPA 69)

Historical

This unit is created in September 1933. It receives its first MORANE MS406 from May 1939. GC I / 3 is the first group to be transformed on the D520 and leaves in December 1939 to the airfield of Cannes-Mandelieu to take in hand the new long-awaited fighter .

He left the operations for this purpose, and is attached to the ZOAA (Alpine Air Operations Zone), to return only May 11, 1940, and expand his record on with his new plane.

The GC I / 3 will finish the campaign in France with a record of 55 Victories sure and 19 probable including 5 sure and 1 probable obtained on Morane-Saulnier MS406, for the loss of 10 pilots killed, 7 wounded and 2 prisoners.

GC I / 3 is finally dissolved in October 1941. GC I / 3 is finally dissolved in October 1941.
