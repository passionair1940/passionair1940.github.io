Golden Book of the 51st Wing
 
1st Squadron (SAL 42)
2nd Squadron (SAL 39)

Historical

Born GB I / 51, he became GBA I / 51 when he joined the Bombardment Assault December 28, 1939.

On April 15, 1940, the 6th Bombardment Brigade and its Wings are disbanded, and GBA I / 51, formerly owned by 51 Wing, is now attached to Group n°19 of the South Bomber Aviation Training Group. East of the ZOAA (Alpine Air Operations Zone).

At the Armistice, only the GBA I / 54 and GBA I / 51 are authorized by the Germans. The 2nd Squadron of the Group thus receives the personnel of the GBA II / 51, dissolved recently.

The group is finally dissolved on December 1st, 1942 on the Orange airfield.
