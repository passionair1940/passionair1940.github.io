Golden Book of the 51st Wing
 
3rd Squadron (SAL 4)
4th Squadron (39eme RAO)

Historical

Born GB II / 51, he became GBA II / 51 when he joined the Bombardment Assault December 15, 1939.

On April 15, 1940, the 6th Bombardment Brigade and its Wings are disbanded, and the GBA II / 51, formerly owned by 51 Wing, is now attached to Group No. 19 of the South Bomber Aviation Training Group. East of the ZOAA (Alpine Air Operations Zone).

At the Armistice, only the GBA I / 54 and GBA I / 51 are authorized by the Germans. The latter Group therefore receives the staff of GBA II / 51, which will be dissolved on August 15, 1940.

The GBA II/51 was equipped since its creation, following aircrafts :

- Potez 540
- Bloch MB210
- Potez 633

The used aircrafts are described in the accessible file by the link below. You will find N ° of series, Registration, date of affectation to the group, and fate of the plane there.
