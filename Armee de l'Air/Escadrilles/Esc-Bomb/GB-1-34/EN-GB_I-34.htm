Historical

Originally, GB I / 34 is a reconnaissance group: GO I / 34. He becomes GB I / 34 on January 1, 1938

On May 10, 1940, GB I / 34 is attached to Group No. 9 of the 1st Air Division of the ZOAN (North Air Operations Area)
