Bloch MB 160
01

Prototype of the Bloch MB 160 which made its first flight on 18 June 1937. It is engaged on 20 August 1937 in the race Istres-Damascus-Paris. The aircraft will finish 7th ... out of 7. Required by the Air Force in 1939, it will make some connections before taking refuge in AFN. The aaircraft disappeared at the end of the war.
Bloch MB 160
1
F-AREO
 First aircraft of the series, which made its first flight on June 23, 1939. It is requisitioned in 1939 by the Air Force and will make connections to the Armistice. Based in Toulouse, it was later used by the Vichy government before being transferred to Germany in 1943, after the invasion of the Free Zone on November 11, 1942.
Bloch MB 160
2
F-AREP
 Second series aircraft which made its first flight in July 1939. It is requisitioned in 1939 by the Air Force and will make connections to the Armistice before taking refuge in AFN. The aircraft disappeared at the end of the war.
