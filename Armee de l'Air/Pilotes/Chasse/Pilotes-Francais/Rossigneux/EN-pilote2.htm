Lieutenant ROSSIGNEUX

Biography

 

Some feats of arm

On May 12, 1940, Lieutenant ROSSIGNEUX in a patrol of GC I/1 attacks three DORNIER DO215. They attack the enemy formation from behind, but the bombers defend themselves fiercely and drop their bombs to flee. If the left winger, touched, leaves his section, the French can not pursue him for lack of ammunition. Victory will be counted as probable to the patrol.

On May 29, 1940, Lieutenant ROSSIGNEUX took part in a mission of the 1st Squadron of GC I/I led by Captain COUTAUD. The patrol intercepts a DORNIER DO17. This one maneuvers to hide in the clouds, but the patrol is waiting for him below and above the cloud layer, and at each reappearance, he is hit to finally dive to the ground, engine burning, and crush near Laon. Victory is awarded to all patrol pilots.
