Staff-Sergeant MOREL

Biography

Staff sergeant MOREL is hurt in Air combat on May 21st, 1940 and June 5th, 1940.

Some feats of arm

September 8th, Sergeant MOREL, belonging to the GC I/1 is sent with Sergeant Tain, of the same Group, to the CEMA of Orléans to prepare the implementation of Bloch MB152

On May 21, 1940, Sergeant MOREL took part in a mission commanded by Commander COUTAUD. As the patrol prepares to attack a formation of enemy bombers, they are attacked by the ME109E escort. Sergeant MOREL's plane was severely hit, and the injured pilot managed to land on the Chantilly property. His landing too long ends in the trees surrounding the field. Sgt. MOREL, shocked, is injured on the hands.

On June 5, 1940, following an aerial combat, Staff-Sergeant MOREL destroyed his Bloch 152 Nb 397 at Landing and was wounded.
