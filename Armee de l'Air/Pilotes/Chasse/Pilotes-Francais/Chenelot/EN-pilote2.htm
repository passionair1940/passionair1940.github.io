Sergeant Philippe CHENELOT

Biography

Philippe CHENELOT was born on 09 September 1914 in Valentigney, in the Doubs.
Sergeant CHENELOT Killed in Air Combat June 06, 1940

Some feats of arm

On June 6, 1940, Sergeant CHENELOT participated in a protection mission for the VIIth Army within a GC II / 1 patrol. Sergeant CHENELOT is separated from his patrol and faces alone 6 MESSERSCHMII BF109E. After having shot one, he is himself touched and must evacuate his aircraft. The German fighters fire on the parachute that ignites: the French pilot crashes on the ground: he is killed instantly.
