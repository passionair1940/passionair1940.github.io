Lieutenant Pierre TREBOD

Biography

Pierre TREBOD was born February 7, 1910 in Bordeaux.

Lieutenant TREBOD was a test pilot at the SNCASO-SNECMA factory in Chatellerault and made many connections between Chatellerault and Bordeaux. He joined GC I / 1 on April 20, 1940. He was killed in air combat on June 03, 1940

 

Some feats of arm

June 3rd, 1940, Germans launched a mission of bombardment on Paris with an important dispositf of Bombers protected by numerous fighters. Lieutenant TREBOD takes off with a triple patrol from GC I/1, but they are immediately affected by one ME 109E. Lieutenant TREBOD is shot down and crashes near Crouy-en-Thelle
