Staff-Sergeant Lucjan SZEMPLINSKI

Biography

Sergeant-Chef Lucjan SZEMPLINSKI was born in 1912. After the Polish defeat, he joined France and first fought at GC I / 145 and then, on June 14, he was assigned to GC I / 1. On June 18, 1940, he left this group with the other seven Polish pilots to join England.

He was killed in 1944.
