Hyp 2

Aircraft assigned to GR I / 52: Shot by Fighter Aircrafts on 21 December 1939.The crew is killed: Adjt LE-SAOULT (Pilot), Adjt-Chef VERON (Observateur) and Sgt MELOT (machine gunner).

Enigma Resolved byMr JC FONTAN (September 2018) :
The second hypothesis is the good one, but this Potez 637 n ° 41 was shot down in Alsace, in Barembach (Bas-Rhin), not in Istres.
This information is confirmed twice:
1 - By a stele installed at Square Forton in Barembach. It bears the names of Saoult, Veron and Melot. It is dedicated to 19 French airmen (Potez 637 of I / 52, 3 men, 21/12/1939), Americans (B-24H "Rough Buddy" of USAAC 707th BS, 9 men on 10/09/44) and English (Lancaster, RAF 207 Sqn, 7 men, 04/12/44) fell in this agglomeration for freedom.
2 - By the website "https://www.aerosteles.net/plan" giving a photo of the stele of barembach, itself referenced by "francecrashes39-45.net" Mr. Daniel Carville.

The pilot Ltt De Chézelle is killed, Sgt Périot managed to land.
