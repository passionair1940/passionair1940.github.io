In this section are listed 393 Airfields used from Mobilization to the Armistice by the 144 units of the French Air Force identified in this site. For each of them, I counted the dates of use of the units that passed through it. Of course, this list is far from exhaustive and will probably never be, but it will be supplemented or modified with the discovery of information.

These Airfields are classified by region (see menu on the left of this page), according to the current administrative division (2017). however, you will find a list of the Airfields in alphabetical order at the bottom of this page.

Airfields are thus distributed by regions and maps will allow you to localize on one hand the region and on the other hand the Airfields within this region.

Gradually, I would also add a more precise location, allowing to visualize the setting-up with regard to the closest city and the outline of Airfields. This part is particularly complex for several reasons:
- Number of grounds disappeared today, it is thus necessary to look in archives to confirm the location. I also lean for it on the very detailed site: " Anciens Aérodromes "
- Under two names of different Airfields, sometimes hides the same Airfield... Indeed, if this one is situated between two close cities, he can be alternately appointed with the name of the one or other one of both cities following sources.
