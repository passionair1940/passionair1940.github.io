Units assigned to Fleet 12FB
(Bombing Fleet)

Commander of the Fleet : Lieutenant-Commander Antoine DE-GAIL

The Fleet was created on June 15, 1940 and was to be composed of the Bombing Squadrons (B-2, B-3, B-4,...) being transformed on the Gleen-Martin 167F purchased from the United States. But only 3B Squadron had time to be equipped before the Armistice, but too late for the flotilla to have any role in the conflict.
