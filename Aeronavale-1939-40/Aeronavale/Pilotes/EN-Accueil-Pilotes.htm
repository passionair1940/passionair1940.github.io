In this section dedicated to the Pilots and Crews of the French Naval Aviation, I wanted to pay tribute to all these men, who will remain unknown for the most part: the Great History will soon forget them, with even more certainty than the members of the Air Force.

They patrolled on long and endless missions over the seas and oceans. They had the courage to face, at the controls of often outdated aircraft, an enemy always superior in number. Too many are those who have given their lives in these unequal battles often waged in total self-denial.

In the different sections, you can find individual cards on some of these men, or the complete lists (will they be one day?) by specialty.

To date, 293 Men are listed in these lists, they are distributed as follows:

Fighter Aviation : 58 men
Bombardment & Torpedo : 100 men
Embarked Seaplanes : 42 men
Exploration & Surveillance : 93 men

These lists will be updated regularly. Your help is of course welcome to help me complete them. .
