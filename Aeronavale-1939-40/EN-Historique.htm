- The 1,000-page mark has been passed!

I had the opportunity to introduce myself in the page similar to this one, in the part of the site dedicated to "The French Air Force". So I will only mention here, the creation of these pages dedicated to the French Naval Aviation.
The idea of creating this part of the site dedicated to the French Naval Aviation was in a corner of my head as soon as the Main Site was online in summer 2016. But in order not to postpone the date of the site, I first wanted to publish the 690 pages of the French Air Force site before adding, in July 2020, the 340 pages on the Naval Aviation: here is the milestone of 1000 pages crossed with this last complement. To this, we must add the equivalent number of pages in English... The historical period treated is however very short: from September 1939 to June 1940.... But the subject is vast and I would probably not have enough of a life to complete it as I would like...

I therefore invite you to discover this site which will be regularly enriched, and if you wish, you can make additions and corrections by writing to me via the "contact me" link.
