Some interesting links to discover :

 

 This site is the work of François Xavier Bibert, son of joseph Bibert, mechanic at GC III / 6. Browsing the different pages that make up this site is a real treat. We "walk" with pleasure with the links proposed that make us discover documents of great wealth
Histavia21.net is a site containing articles on the history of aviation in the Burgundy and Franche-Comté regions. It contains a list of aircraft accidents in the region and much more to discover.

Here again, an extremely detailed site, with multiple links, photos, insignia, ... Not to be missed !

This site traces the life and career of René MUNIER, fighter pilot, fallen on 15 May 1940. It is François IUNG, his grandson who is the actor of this site very complete and detailed. They include commemorations, testimonies, the GC II / 1 Group ... so do not miss it!

This site lists all types of French aircraft manufactured since the early days of Aviation. Some manufacturers were totally unknown to me. See also the register of French aircraft registrations.
This site, even if it does not speak exclusively of aviation is very interesting. It lists the French soldiers of all weapons and of all periods buried in our national cemeteries, and one can thus find there the French Airmen died during the period 1939/1940
This site traces the history of the French, Navy and Air Force squadrons:
- In 1939 / 1940
- Between 1940 and 1945 in the Free French forces
You will discover here a collection of beautiful Squadrons badges
